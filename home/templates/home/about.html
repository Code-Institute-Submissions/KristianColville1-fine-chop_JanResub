Our philosophy

It is our pleasure to serve you authentic Asian food at FineChop Restaurant. We deliver to you the flavours, textures, and aromas of Asian cuisine.

Enjoy the flavours of the delectable meals that our crew from Thailand, China, Korea and Japan have worked so hard to prepare. Many local customers have fallen in love with the restaurant's savoury, delectable food, and they return frequently. It is our mission to frequently improve our standards by listening to our customers taste buds.

You may enjoy our delicious, authentic Asian food in a lively, modern setting for fair prices. This is how we ate dinner at home: we brought FineChop from the streets of Asia to the countryside of Ireland. We value the artistic expression of our culture and are confident that it will please the senses. Therefore, close your eyes, pay attention, take in the aromas of Asia, and most importantly, taste them.
